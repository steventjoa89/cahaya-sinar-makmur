Our Goal: Give the best quality products for our customers.

PT. Cahaya Sinar Makmur is one of the leading companies engaged in the importer and distributor of chemicals located in Jakarta, Indonesia.

Established in May 1989, PT Cahaya Sinar Makmur has a vision to provide the best quality chemicals to support domestically produced goods, where the quality produced can compete with goods from abroad at competitive prices.

We also has a concept to "be a good partner with your company". Maintaining the quality of goods and providing the best service is also one of our base points to be the best for our customers. The basis for the success of our strategy is based on our seven pillars provided by our highly qualified and motivated team.

We always strive to the maximum to achieve the goal of the founder of our company, namely "achieving a good relationship between our company and customers", as evidenced by the satisfaction of our customers for more than 30 years and we feel like a big family in achieving a shared dream.

PT. Cahaya Sinar Makmur at a glance.

PT. Cahaya Sinar Makmur is responsible for the Indonesia business for industrial chemical trading, with over multiple customers and employess in Indonesia island. The company focuses on the importing, trading and distribution of general industrial chemicals. Furthermore, the company aspires to be one of the best in Indonesia markets. Our chemicals products also can be used in plastic, rubber, glass, food industries.

PT. Cahaya Sinar Makmur is one the Indonesia chemical traders that trades high-volume of chemical distribution. We also have three different segments for our customers, such as factory, distributor and personal uses. PT. Cahaya Sinar Makmur is continually expanding our market throughout the entire nations in Indonesia archipelago.

As sustainability is one of the basic principle in our strategy and for our company itself, this means creating long lasting relationships between our stakeholders: customers, employees, business partners and society. The basis for this as we can see from our ancestor business strategy.
